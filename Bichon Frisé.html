Bichon Frisé

The Bichon Frisé’s coat is hypoallergenic and almost never sheds.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Height: 9.5–11.5 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 12–15 years
  • Habitat: Companion animals (family homes worldwide)

Bichon Frisés are cheerful, playful, and affectionate dogs known for their white, puffy coats and gentle personalities. They are great with children and other pets, making them a popular choice for families. Their hypoallergenic coats are a big plus for allergy sufferers!
